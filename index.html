Sanya

Sanya, Hainan, which is located in a beautiful island in the south of China

Beach

The beach in Sanya is a wonderful place The sand here is soft, and it feels as comfortable as a carpet when you walk on it. The sea water is clear and the sun shines on it, and the sea is blue and glowing. The sunset in Sanya Bay is really beautiful. The beach is especially suitable for walking. Watching the sun slowly fall into the sea and enjoying the sea breeze is super relaxing. There are fewer tourists and a good environment. If you like water sports, go directly to Wuzhizhou Island. There are diving, motorboats, and surfing. The sea water is as blue as if the filter is turned on too large. It's really beautiful! Young people and surfing enthusiasts usually go to Houhai Village, where the best waves are. Many people surf during the day and drink and chat on the beach during the night, which creates a special atmosphere.

Hotel

Hotels in Sanya are simply a holiday paradise! Espcially there are many sea view to super those luxurious hotels, there are so many choices. If you want to enjoy the top level, you won't miss them out. such as Atlantis Aquarium and Water World, Ritz-Carlton's they own private beach, and Mandarin Oriental's quiet villas. Each one is a resort that is so exquisite that people don't want to leave, staying in the hotel, you can see the sea by opening the window, If you like swimming, you also can use their swimming pool. go to the sea to watch the waves at any time, and enjoy the cozy experience over there. Whether you want to lie in the infinity pool to watch the sea, or stay in the balcony to stare the sea breeze, the hotels in Sanya can make people live comfortably, have fun and truly enjoy the fun of vacation!

Afternoon Tea

Afternoon tea in Sanya is so relaxing! Sitting on the terrace by the sea at the Ritz-Carlton, drinking a cup of tea, paired with soft scones and exquisite snacks, and relaxing with the sea breeze. The Edition in Rosewood Hotel is also great, with fruit and small desserts and also with tropical fruits, the sun shines on your body, it feels like a scene from a vacation movie. There is also something special, afternoon tea in Mandarin Oriental Hotel is like drinking tea on a cliff, with the hot steam in the teacup and the blue of the sea complementing each other, super cozy! Whether you are chatting with friends or enjoying it alone, afternoon tea in Sanya is a good time to realx yourself.
